OUR WORK

Basundhara Agrotech has achieved significant milestones since its focusing on agricultural innovation, farmer empowerment, and sustainable practices. From promoting mustard and honey production to adopting advanced farming techniques, procuring machinery, andestablishing agro-input retail and honey processing facilities, the company has steadily progressed toward its vision of transforming agriculture.

2021

Installed 300 honey bee boxes,

cultivated mustard on 27 hectares,

and procured 100 quintals of mustard seeds fromfarmers.

2022

Farmers adopted machine transplantation for winter paddy under the APART project,

With guidance from Krishi Vigyan Kendra scientists.

Purchased a tractor, rotavator, and paddy reaper with 90% subsidy

Through the Village Level Farm Machinery Bank Scheme.

2023

Opened a retail shop for fertilizers and agrochemicals

2024

Inaugurated a Honey Processing Unit, strengthening value addition

And market opportunities.
