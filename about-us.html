History

Basundhara Agrotech, founded in 2021, is a farmer cooperative company supported by NABARD and partnered with Krishi Vigyan Kendra, Lakhimpur. Starting with 200 farmer shareholders, it focuses on sustainable agriculture, particularly mustard and honey. Governed by five directors, the company empowers farmers and promotes innovation in agricultural practices.

VISION

Basundhara Agrotech envisions empowering farmers through sustainable agriculture, innovation, and collaboration. Focused on mustard and honey, it strives to enhance productivity, ensure profitability, and promote eco-friendly practices. By fostering rural developmenut and selfreliance, the company aims to transform agriculture into a thriving, sustainable industry that benefits farmers and the region.

MISSION

The mission of Basundhara Agrotech Producer Company is to enhance the livelihoods of farmers by providing them with modern agricultural resources, training, and market access. The company is dedicated to promoting sustainable farming practices, increasing productivity, and supporting the cultivation of mustard and honey while ensuring economic growth and environmental conservation.
